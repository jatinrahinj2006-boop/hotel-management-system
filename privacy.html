Privacy Policy

At Aurum Hotels, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or make a reservation, we collect the following information:

  • Name — To identify you and personalize your experience
  • Email address — For booking confirmations and communication
  • Phone number — For booking-related updates and support

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used exclusively for:

  • Processing and managing your room reservations
  • Sending booking confirmations and updates
  • Communicating important information about your stay
  • Providing customer support when needed

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Your password is securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data remains strictly confidential and is used solely for the purposes outlined above.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Please contact us if you wish to make any changes to your account details.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to our support team.
